A robust call management system was implemented to streamline rental operations and enhance communication between property managers and tenants. Leveraging Telnyx integration, the platform supports seamless inbound and outbound calling with features such as call routing, mute/unmute, hold, decline, ringing, and call recording for multiple agents. The solution is designed to improve operational efficiency and deliver a smooth, reliable communication experience across the rental lifecycle.
